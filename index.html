Overview

"Never Let Me Go" is a dystopian science fiction novel by Kazuo Ishiguro. It explores the lives of three friends—Kathy, Tommy, and Ruth—who discover unsettling truths about their existence and purpose in life.

Characters

  • Kathy H.
  • Tommy
  • Ruth

Themes

The novel delves into themes of identity, humanity, love, loss, and the ethical implications of scientific advancements.
