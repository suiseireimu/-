Never Let Me Go

By Alan and Isabella

Setting Characters Themes Important Moments Questions Additional

Setting

The novel is set in a dystopian England, featuring locations such as Hailsham, the Cottages, and medical recovery centers.

Characters

  • Kathy H.: The protagonist and narrator.
  • Tommy: Kathy's friend with a temper but a kind heart.
  • Ruth: Kathy's manipulative but significant friend.
  • Miss Lucy: A teacher who reveals truths about their purpose.

Themes

  • Identity: Exploring what it means to be human.
  • Ethics: The moral questions surrounding cloning and organ donation.
  • Nostalgia: The role of memory in shaping identity.

Important Moments

Key moments in the novel include:

  • Students at Hailsham discover their purpose as organ donors.
  • Kathy, Ruth, and Tommy visit Madame to explore the idea of "deferrals."
  • The ultimate realization of the futility of escaping their fate.
